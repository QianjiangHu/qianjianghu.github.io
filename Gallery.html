Human Induced Pluripotent Stem (hiPSC)

hiPSC

These hiPSC were generated from human skin fibroblast cells. hiPSC hold promise in the field of regenerative medicine. Because they can propagate indefinitely, as well as give rise to every other cell type in the body. It provides new opportunities to create in vitro models of healthy and diseased human tissues.

Generating hiPSC in microfludic chips

The hiPSC were generated in microfludic chips, which significatly improved the generating efficiency. Comparing with other technology, hiPSC generated from microfludic chips showed higher pluripotency. Images here show the embryo body formation and Cardiomyocyte-like Cells with Beating Rhythm from hiPSC.
